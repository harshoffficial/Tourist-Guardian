# Step-by-Step: Enable Maps SDK in Google Cloud Console

## Current Screen: Dashboard

### Step 1: Change Project (if needed)
- Look at the top-left dropdown showing "My First Project"
- Click it and select "tourist-guardian-a0d19"
- If you don't see it, click "Select a project" and search for it

### Step 2: Go to APIs & Services
- In the "Quick access" section (middle of the page)
- Click on the **"APIs & Services"** card
- This will take you to the APIs page

### Step 3: Enable Maps SDK for Android
- In the APIs & Services page, click on **"Library"** in the left sidebar
- Search for "Maps SDK for Android"
- Click on "Maps SDK for Android" in the results
- Click the blue **"Enable"** button
- Wait for it to enable (usually takes a few seconds)

### Step 4: Verify
- You should see a green checkmark or "API enabled" message
- The button should now say "Manage" instead of "Enable"

## That's it! Your map should work now.
